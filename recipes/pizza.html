Pizza

Description:

This is some tips and tricks to make the best at home pizza that you can.

Ingredients:

  • Marinara Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Basil
  • Self Rising Flour
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 F.
  2. Grate the Mozzarella Cheese.
  3. Mix the flour, water, and salt in a bowl.
